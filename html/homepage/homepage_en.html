Use "Expand my results" to perform a more comprehensive search.

Results produced by "Expand my results" may not be available or owned by Western Theological Seminary.

Use an asterisk * as a wildcard to replace one or more characters.

Examples: cat* finds cat, cats, catalog, etc.; wom*n finds woman and women

Enclosing a phrase in quotation marks (" ") finds an exact match.

Example: "Puritan Theology"

Boolean Operators fine-tune your searches by using the operators "AND", "OR", and "NOT" to combine keywords to broaden or narrow your search. You must always type these operators in capital letters.

Some online resources are not discoverable in Primo. Also consult our other Online Databases .

How does this work?

Primo provides simple, one-stop searching for books and e-books, videos, articles, digital media, and more.

Primo also helps you manage your research. Sign-in in order to:

  • Renew books
  • Request that books be placed on hold for you
  • Create favorites lists
  • View full search results. (Some databases show results only when you're signed in.)

Suggestion:
Install Zotero to manage your citations

 What am I searching?
Books+Media+Articles — This is the broadest search. Find books, e-books, journals, DVDs and more in Cook Library, as well as online articles to which the Cook Library subscribes.
Library Catalog — Find books and more at the Cook Library.

EBSCOhost — Find articles in Atla Religion Database, Old Testament Abstracts, New Testament Abstracts and other EBSCOhost databases.

You must be signed into Primo and a current Western Theological Seminary student, faculty member, or employee to use this resource.
Articles & more — Find online articles to which the Cook Library subscribes.
Course Reserves — Find items which are on reserve by course name, course ID, instructor and department.
WorldCat — Search WorldCat for materials not owned by Cook Library.
Special Collections — Find items in Cook Library Special Collections.
